Profile

I am a trainer with strong scientific background, owner of Cztery Bity. I teach programming in C, C++ and Python at various levels of advancement. I have MA degree in Physics and PhD in Materials Sciences, and I was working as a researcher for 11 years. My experience as a programmer is based of my need (as a scientist) for data processing and visualization. As a trainer I have worked (either directly or through other companies) for Nokia, Intel, Autodesk, Ericpol, Tieto, Interia.pl and others. In my scientific career I was working with X-ray diffraction, using my home Institute lab equipment, visiting SAXS Beamline in Elettra Lab and Petra III Beamline at DESY. In my free time I'm involved in Software Carpentry foundation.
